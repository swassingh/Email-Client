# PM Vision — NovaMail v2 (POC)

## Vision Statement
NovaMail is the next-generation email workspace where users do not manage an inbox — they execute outcomes.

## Product Thesis
Chronological inboxes are broken for modern work. NovaMail replaces passive reading with active lanes:
- **Focus Now**: critical, high-leverage decisions.
- **Quick Wins**: low-friction actions to rapidly clear queue.
- **FYI Stream**: non-urgent context retained without distraction.

## Week-1 POC Goals
1. Modern, executive-ready interface quality.
2. Demonstrable external email delivery to Gmail via SMTP.
3. New inbox paradigm that is visibly different from traditional clients.

## Success Criteria
- Manager can see immediate visual differentiation from standard inbox tools.
- PM architecture email can be delivered externally with valid SMTP credentials.
- Team artifacts are aligned for Week-2 scale build.
